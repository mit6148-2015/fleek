What is on fleek?

fleek is a service that allows you to solve problems anywhere at any time. Using fleek, you can hone your math and science skills on our 6000+ problem database. We track your statistics and tell you how to improve. blah blah blah make this sound cooler

fleek is #stacked, built using MongoDB, Mongoose, Node.js, Express, Passport, and Angular.js. Our data is generated from crawling various math and science competition websites, and our scrapers are written in Python. We also use MathJax to render LaTeX and Asymptote to generate diagrams. Our splash page map runs on the amCharts library, and our programmers run on sleep deprivation.

The fleek source code can be found on GitHub.
